\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Instances of {\ttfamily \mbox{\hyperlink{classjavazoom_1_1jl_1_1decoder_1_1_bitstream_exception}{Bitstream\+Exception}}} are thrown when operations on a {\ttfamily \mbox{\hyperlink{classjavazoom_1_1jl_1_1decoder_1_1_bitstream}{Bitstream}}} fail. 

The exception provides details of the exception condition in two ways\+: 
\begin{DoxyEnumerate}
\item as an error-\/code describing the nature of the error ~\newline

\item as the {\ttfamily Throwable} instance, if any, that was thrown indicating that an exceptional condition has occurred. 
\end{DoxyEnumerate}
