Detailed Description

Bitstream unit test. It matches test.mp3 properties to test.mp3.properties expected results. As we don't ship test.mp3, you have to generate your own test.mp3.properties Uncomment out = System.out; in setUp() method to generated it on stdout from your own MP3 file.
